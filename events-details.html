2022 Graduation Ceremony

Karumo TTI will hold its 2022 th Graduation Ceremony on Friday 22th July, 2022 from 9:00 A.M at the Main Campus Graduands.
You are requested to join any of the virtual page links to be provided in the invitation card and the website by 8:30 A.M.
You are all highly welcome.
All Graduands are requested to check on the Nottice board for name inclusion, name spellings, order of names (as in our records) and Registration Numbers.
No certificate or Official Academic Transcript shall be printed with any initials.
Please note corrections on these documents draw service charges.

ACADEMIC ATTIRE

Academic attire will be issued to graduands from the offices of the respective School Deans from 19th to 22th July, 2022.
Graduands will be required to leave behind their National ID upon issuance of the Academic attire. The ID will be given back upon return of the attire. The graduation gown MUST be returned on or before 29t August, 2022 at 5.00 p.m.
Any delay in returning the gown will attract a daily charge of Kshs.500/- and any loss or damage to the attire will be charged at cost.
