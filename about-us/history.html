About Karumo Technical Training Institute

 Read More

Brief History

Karumo Technical Training Institute was established in 2015 under certificate of registration number RC/0006/2014 in recognition of the fact that Technology is a part of everyday life. KTTI is managed by a Board of Governors (BOG) under the direction of the Ministry of Education. It operates under the TVET Act (2013) of the Laws of Kenya as well as Constitution of Kenya 2010, KNEC Act, PSC Act, KASNEB ACT, Policy guidelines among others.

Our Vision

Karumo TTI is a Technical Institute in Tigania West in Meru County. The Institute is at the periphery of Isiolo County, a region with high potential in tourism, industrial, renewable energy potential among others.
